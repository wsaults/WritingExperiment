# Species Profile: Pachori

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Sympathetic
**Biology**: Mammalian (pachyderm)

---

## Physical Description

Elephant-like humanoids with trunk-like noses and large ears. 2.5-3m tall. Gray, wrinkled skin. Never forget.

---

## Cultural Overview

Memory-keepers. Oral tradition spanning millions of years. Grief and joy expressed through elaborate ceremonies.

---

## Narrative Use

Historians; witnesses to ancient events; emotional weight; unstoppable when charging.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Pachori's trunk curled thoughtfully. 'I remember,' they said. 'I remember everything. Including what your species has already forgotten.'"
