# Species Profile: Goreth

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Neutral
**Biology**: Mammalian (primate)

---

## Physical Description

Massive ape-like beings, 2-2.5m tall. Silver-backed leaders, darker subordinates. Powerful builds, surprisingly gentle hands.

---

## Cultural Overview

Strength-hierarchy with strict honor codes. Respect earned through combat or demonstrated wisdom. Protect their troops absolutely.

---

## Narrative Use

Mercenary companies; honorable opponents; surprisingly philosophical warriors.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Goreth studied me with ancient eyes. 'Small,' they rumbled. 'But small things have surprised me before. Show me what you are.'"
