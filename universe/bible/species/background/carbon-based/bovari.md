# Species Profile: Bovari

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Sympathetic
**Biology**: Mammalian (bovine)

---

## Physical Description

Large, stocky humanoids with horns, broad faces, and powerful builds. 2m average. Herbivores with patient demeanors.

---

## Cultural Overview

Agricultural society. Value growth, patience, and community. Slow to anger but devastating when provoked.

---

## Narrative Use

Farmers in peaceful scenes; stampede combat; gentle giants; steadfast allies.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Bovari's nostrils flared. 'We do not start fights,' they said quietly. 'We finish them.'"
