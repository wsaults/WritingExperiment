# Species Profile: Crustari

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Neutral
**Biology**: Crustacean

---

## Physical Description

Crab-like beings with hard shells, multiple legs, and powerful pincers. 1-1.5m tall when upright. Shells often decorated or armored.

---

## Cultural Overview

Molt-based society—status resets with each shell-shedding. Masters of salvage and underwater construction.

---

## Narrative Use

Engineers; salvage operations; underwater combat; armor/shell crafting.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Crustari clicked their pincers—a gesture of greeting or threat, I couldn't tell. 'New shell, new deal. What was promised before... was another me.'"
