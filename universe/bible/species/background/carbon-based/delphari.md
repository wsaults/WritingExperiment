# Species Profile: Delphari

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Sympathetic
**Biology**: Cetacean (dolphin-like)

---

## Physical Description

Aquatic humanoids with smooth gray skin, elongated skulls, and echolocation organs. Graceful in water, awkward on land. 1.8m average.

---

## Cultural Overview

Pod-based society with complex acoustic language. Playful, curious, sometimes dangerously so.

---

## Narrative Use

Ocean exploration; sonar/detection capabilities; comic relief (pranks); unexpected allies.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Delphari's clicks resolved into words through my translator: 'You move funny. Like a rock that wants to be a fish. We like you.'"
