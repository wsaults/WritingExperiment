# Species Profile: Corvex

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Neutral
**Biology**: Avian (corvid-like)

---

## Physical Description

Sleek black-feathered humanoids with sharp beaks and wing-arms. Average 1.6m tall. Iridescent plumage shifts purple-green in light.

---

## Cultural Overview

Collectors and archivists. Obsessed with shiny objects, which they've elevated to a complex trade economy.

---

## Narrative Use

Merchants specializing in rare artifacts; information brokers; scene dressing for markets.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Corvex trader's head tilted, one eye fixed on the trinket in my hand. 'Interesting,' they croaked. 'Very interesting.'"
