# Species Profile: Felixar

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Wildcard
**Biology**: Mammalian (feline)

---

## Physical Description

Lithe cat-like humanoids, 1.7m average. Large eyes with vertical pupils, retractable claws, patterned fur (spotted, striped, or solid).

---

## Cultural Overview

Fiercely independent. No centralized government—each Felixar is their own nation. Form temporary alliances only.

---

## Narrative Use

Assassins; scouts; unreliable allies who might help or hinder based on whim.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Felixar smiled—all teeth, no warmth. 'I'll help you. Today. Tomorrow is a different question.'"
