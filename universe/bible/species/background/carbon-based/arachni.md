# Species Profile: Arachni

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Hostile
**Biology**: Arachnid

---

## Physical Description

Eight-limbed beings with chitinous exoskeletons. Multiple eyes (usually six). Range from 1-2m. Web-spinners with natural silk production.

---

## Cultural Overview

Web-based architecture and communication. Territorial. View most other species as either threats or prey.

---

## Narrative Use

Dungeon hazards; trap-makers; genuinely creepy encounters; silk as valuable commodity.
