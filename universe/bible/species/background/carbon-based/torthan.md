# Species Profile: Torthan

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Neutral
**Biology**: Reptilian (chelonian)

---

## Physical Description

Heavily armored humanoids with shell-like backs. Slow-moving but nearly invulnerable. 1.4m tall but can retract to half that.

---

## Cultural Overview

Patience as religion. Decisions take generations. Their word, once given, is unbreakable because changing it would take too long.

---

## Narrative Use

Unbreakable shields; immovable obstacles; contracts that last forever; comedy of slowness.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Torthan considered my proposal. Three hours later, they blinked. 'We... are... considering.'"
