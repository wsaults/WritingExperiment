# Species Profile: Vixari

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Sympathetic
**Biology**: Mammalian (rodent-like)

---

## Physical Description

Small (0.8-1m), with large ears, long whiskers, and quick movements. Fur in earth tones. Constantly twitching, observing.

---

## Cultural Overview

Scavengers and recyclers. Find value in what others discard. Vast underground warrens where nothing is wasted.

---

## Narrative Use

Information from the margins; salvage specialists; allies in desperate circumstances; underestimated.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Vixari's nose twitched. 'Throw-aways know things the kept-ones don't. You want to know what the big ones miss?'"
