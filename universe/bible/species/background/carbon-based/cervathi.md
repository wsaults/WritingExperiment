# Species Profile: Cervathi

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Neutral
**Biology**: Mammalian (cervine)

---

## Physical Description

Deer-like humanoids with branching antlers (shed seasonally). Graceful, quick, alert. 1.8m average. Large, watchful eyes.

---

## Cultural Overview

Flight-over-fight philosophy. Masters of evasion and territory knowledge. Antler-size indicates age and wisdom.

---

## Narrative Use

Guides; scouts; prey in hunting scenarios; graceful allies; seasonal events (antler shedding).

---

## Quick Hook

"The Cervathi's ears swiveled independently, tracking sounds I couldn't hear. 'Something comes,' they whispered. 'We should not be here when it arrives.'"
