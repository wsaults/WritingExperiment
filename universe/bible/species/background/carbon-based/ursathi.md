# Species Profile: Ursathi

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Neutral
**Biology**: Mammalian (ursine)

---

## Physical Description

Massive bear-like beings, 2.5-3m tall when standing. Dense fur ranging from brown to white. Small ears, powerful limbs.

---

## Cultural Overview

Hibernation-based society. Work intensely for half the cycle, sleep the other half. Patient traders who never rush deals.

---

## Narrative Use

Bodyguards; slow but inevitable pursuit; comic relief (sleepy giants); intimidating presence.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Ursathi yawned, revealing teeth that could crush my skull. 'We discuss terms... after my nap.'"
