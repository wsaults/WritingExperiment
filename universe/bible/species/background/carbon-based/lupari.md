# Species Profile: Lupari

**Tier**: 3 (Background)
**Classification**: Wildcard
**Biology**: Mammalian (canine)

---

## Physical Description

Wolf-like humanoids with keen senses and pack structure. 1.7-1.9m tall. Fur colors vary by region. Prominent fangs.

---

## Cultural Overview

Pack-loyalty above all. Will die for pack members without hesitation. Outsiders must prove themselves to earn trust.

---

## Narrative Use

Tracking; pack tactics in combat; loyalty tests; allies earned through respect.

---

## Quick Hook

"The Lupari alpha's eyes glowed in the darkness. 'You are not pack,' they growled. 'But pack can be... earned.'"
